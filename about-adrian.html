Hi, my name is Adrian.

I am a UX/Product Designer with frontend development skills. I create cohesive and functional designs based on user research and applied through UX design frameworks.

I am currently based in Irvine, CA and I create cohesive and functional designs based on user research and applied through UX design frameworks. Over the past 8 years, I've built a successful career in sales. It's been an incredible journey during which I've gained valuable insights into customer relationships and my developing my sales skills. However, I've always been intrigued by research and improving business systems.

After doing someonlineresearch,IdiscoveredthatUXdesign incorporates these aspects of empathy, design, and research into a field that focuses on enhancing the user experience. This realization sparked a desire for me to transition into UX design.

In order to acquire a new skill such as UX, I recognized the need for a systematic approach to learning and enhancing my UX skills. After researching various educational institutions, I ultimately selected CareerFoundry because of their well-organized courses and personalized mentorship program.

As I was working full-time, I had to dedicate my evenings and weekends to learning UX on a part- time basis. Although it was challenging, it proved to be a worthwhile journey for me. After completing the program in 11 months, I am proud of all the skills I have acquired and my transition into a UX designer. When I am not working or thinking of UX Design, I love to travel, learn new languages, and spend time with friends and family.
